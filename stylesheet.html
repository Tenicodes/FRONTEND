Me, Myself & I

Fatihat Ajoke Yakubu is a Software Developer, Designer and an Entrepreneur. She is also a Law Student of the Prestigious Faculty of Law, University of Lagos, Nigeria. She seeks to create impactful changes and believes there are no limits to our accomplishments as individuals.


She is a changemaker, a creative and intelligent young woman, a problem solver and an active volunteer who seeks to welcome her self-development, career advancement and the development of the society at large.


She has her areas of interest spread across software development, Sustainable Development Goals, Policy formulation, Taxation, Content creation, Business Development among others.


When she is not brainstorming on various global solutions and how technology and policies could eradicate some major issues, she is either taking an online course, working on a research or listening to music.
